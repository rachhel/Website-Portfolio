01 - Overview

A brand built from scratch.

The Sustainable Innovation Network needed a cohesive brand identity from the ground up - logo, brand book, and website - to establish a strong and memorable presence. The challenge was aligning visuals and messaging with their values and target audience while ensuring consistency across all platforms.

This project offered the chance to create a unified, impactful brand that set the foundation for the organization's growth - communicating their story and connecting with their audience effectively.

03 - Process

Research, collaboration, iteration.

The process began with understanding the competitive landscape and meeting with the client to align on vision, values, and goals - then moved into logo ideation, sitemap, and moodboard development.

Step 01
Research & Discovery
Competitor analysis, client meetings, brainstorming logo ideations, and creating the sitemap and moodboard.
Step 02
Ideation & Feedback
Design critiques, iterating on lo-fi wireframes, and finalizing the brand book with team and client input.
Step 03
Build & Launch
Full-stack development of the final website using HTML, CSS, JS, and Git - deployed live on Netlify.
