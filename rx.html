01 — Overview

Where Seoul meets the world.

RX Energy is a cross-cultural brand designed to resonate with both Asian and Western audiences. The challenge was to craft innovative branding that seamlessly bridges these cultural contexts — standing out in a competitive energy drink market while feeling authentic to both worlds.

By embracing cross-cultural influences, RX Energy pioneers a unique, inclusive identity that blends traditional Korean elements with modern design — fresh, relatable, and globally appealing.

02 — The Why

Redefining the energy drink market.

RX Energy redefines the energy drink market by blending the rich flavors and traditions of Asian and Western cultures into a unique, health-conscious beverage. Crafted with natural ingredients that promote probiotics and essential vitamins, it offers a refreshing energy boost tailored to the modern, active lifestyle.

A brand that feels fresh, relatable, and culturally authentic — blending tradition with modernity for a global audience.

04 — Brand

An identity built on cultural fusion.

The RX Energy brand mission is to provide a refreshing energy boost with natural ingredients, probiotics, and vitamins — all while blending the rich flavors and traditions of Asian and Western cultures into a unique, health-conscious beverage.

05 — Final Design

Three flavors, one vision.

Each RX Energy can features a distinct color palette reflecting its unique flavor. The backdrop showcases a hand-sketched skyline of Korea's iconic landmarks — from traditional architecture to Seoul Tower — blending cultural heritage with modernity.

A dynamic white stroke symbolizes energy and movement, while three key benefits — green tea caffeine, vitamins, and probiotics — are prominently displayed on each can.

Flavor 01
Lychee
A sweet, floral taste with a hint of citrus, capturing the essence of this beloved Asian fruit. Refreshing and delicate, perfect for those seeking an exotic yet balanced energy boost.
Flavor 02
Muscat
A vibrant, juicy sweetness reminiscent of fresh grapes, paired with subtle floral undertones. Crisp and aromatic, a sophisticated energy boost that delights the palate.
Flavor 03
Orange
Zesty citrus notes delivering a refreshing balance of tangy and sweet. Bright and invigorating, a classic, revitalizing energy boost perfect for any time of day.
06 — Marketing

Immersive pop-up experiences.

Embracing Korea's renowned pop-up store culture, RX Energy launches with a unique immersive experience, each flavor showcased in its own dedicated pop-up room, reflecting the distinct personality and design of that flavor.

Pop-up 01
Lychee Room
Continuous with the color and theme of the lychee fruit, a delicate, floral space that immerses visitors in the flavor experience.
Pop-up 02
Muscat Room
Vibrant and eye-catching, a room that resembles the muscat flavor's lush, grape-forward character.
Pop-up 03
Orange Room
A burst of energy, just like the zesty flavor itself. Bright, bold, and impossible to miss.
